## Special features

The letters are inspired by a page from the book (late 19th century) Dessins de broderie - Stickmuster buch Heft 185

Contrarily to Abécédaire, Abécédaire Small **MUST** be embroidered with thread and needle smaller than usual.
A USA 8 (EUR 60) size needle, and 60WT thread **MUST** be used.
